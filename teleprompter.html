Cold Open - Kitchen Disaster

Good morning, I'm your host, and welcome to "Cooking with Chaos!"

[smile confidently while smoke alarm beeps faintly in background]

Today we're making what I call "Surprise Soufflé" - it's a surprise because even I don't know what's going to happen!

[hold up what appears to be a charcoal brick]

This used to be breakfast.

Segment 1: Kitchen Philosophy

[lean in conspiratorially]

You know, people always ask me, "How do you burn water?" Well, first you need dedication...

[pause dramatically, another smoke alarm joins the first]

And apparently, you need to forget about the kettle for 3 hours.

Breaking News Interruption

[suddenly serious, looking off-camera]

Ladies and gentlemen, we interrupt this cooking show to bring you breaking news...

[pause, listen to earpiece that doesn't exist]

My kitchen is on fire. Again.

Damage Assessment

[coughing slightly, waving smoke away]

The good news is, we've discovered a new cooking method: pyrotechnics!

The bad news is, my insurance company has me on speed dial.

[fire extinguisher sound effect in background]

That's my sous chef, Bob. He's also a volunteer firefighter. What are the odds?

Viewer Mail Segment

[holding obviously fake letter]

Here's a letter from viewer Sarah in Toledo: "Dear Chaos Chef, how do you make toast?"

[look directly at camera, dead serious]

Sarah, I don't.

[pause for effect]

I make carbon sculptures that used to be bread.

Cooking Tip of the Day

[pointing enthusiastically at camera]

Remember folks, cooking is 10% skill, 20% ingredients, and 70% knowing where your fire extinguisher is!

[another alarm starts beeping]

Speaking of which...

Emergency Exit

[backing away from camera slowly]

Well, that's all the time we have for today's show!

[louder alarms, sounds of panic off-camera]

Remember to like and subscribe, and maybe call the fire department!

[shouting over noise]

This has been "Cooking with Chaos," where every meal is an adventure!

Credits Roll

[running off-camera, Bob chasing with fire extinguisher]

Next week: We'll attempt pancakes!

[explosion sound effect]

Or not!
